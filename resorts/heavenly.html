Heavenly Mountain Resort is a ski resort located on the California-Nevada border in South Lake Tahoe. It has 97 runs and 30 lifts that are spread between California and Nevada and four base facilities. The resort has 4,800 acres within its permit area, with approximately 33% currently developed for skiing, boasting the highest elevation of the Lake Tahoe area resorts with a peak elevation of 10,067 ft, and a peak lift-service elevation of 10,040 ft.

At Heavenly Mountain Resort, skiers and snowboarders alike are thrilled with varied terrain that spans the California-Nevada border and includes rolling glades, terrain parks and challenging technical steeps. Ride from state to state on the area's most skiable terrain and reap the benefits of the largest snowmaking system on the West Coast. A Lake Tahoe ski vacation at Heavenly offers something for everyone. Beginners can get comfortable on our groomed runs while more seasoned riders can explore our back-country-like canyons, tree runs and terrain parks. Come explore all the winter fun at Heavenly Mountain!

Surrounded by some of the best summer recreation in the country, Heavenly Mountain Resort offers many warm-weather activities including hiking, mountain biking, backpacking , fishing, hot air balloon rides, and rock climbing. Take advantage of Lake Tahoe with kayaking, stand up paddleboarding, water skiing, a scenic boat cruise and more.
